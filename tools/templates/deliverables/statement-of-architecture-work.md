# Statement of Architecture Work

**Deliverable — a packaged, signed work product.** Unlike an artifact (a catalog/matrix/map), a deliverable is the document a sponsor reviews and signs; it mostly *assembles* artifacts plus a short synthesis. The SoAW scopes and authorises an AEAF engagement. (TOGAF deliverable; AEAF folds most deliverables into artifacts, so keep this thin and link out.)

## Sections

| Section | What to record / assemble |
|---|---|
| **Engagement & sponsor** | What is being architected; the sponsor and accountable executive. |
| **Scope** | In/out of scope; which capabilities and agents; which phases of the method are run (tailoring). |
| **Drivers & constraints** | Why now; budget, time, regulatory constraints. |
| **Agentic scope** *(+I)* | Whether agents are in scope (if yes, Phase 6 + runtime loop are mandatory). |
| **Deliverables produced** | The deliverables this engagement will produce (Vision, ADD, Roadmap, Contract…). |
| **Roles & governance** | Who decides; the architecture board; the gate. |
| **Risks & assumptions** | Engagement-level risks. |
| **Sign-off** | Sponsor + architecture board signatures and date. |

**Keep it thin.** The SoAW points to the artifacts and deliverables that carry the detail; it does not duplicate them.
