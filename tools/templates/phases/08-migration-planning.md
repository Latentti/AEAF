# Phase 8 · Migration Planning

**Purpose.** Sequencing, dependencies, risk, business case. **ADM analogue:** F. **Agentic addition:** plan **frontier moves** (re-allocations) and the **human transition** (reskilling, role change) explicitly. (→ Book 1 §9, §16; Book 2 §17.3.)

## Inputs
- Work packages (Phase 7); the frontier map and its projected moves (Phase 2).

## Steps
1. Document sequencing, dependencies, risk, and the business case.
2. Plan **frontier moves**: each intended re-allocation of work from human to agent, with its **trigger and assurance precondition**. **[+I]**
3. Plan the **human transition**: reskilling and role change for people whose work moves to agents. **[+I]**

## Produces
- Frontier-move sequencing (with assurance preconditions) · autonomy-graduation gates · human transition plan · business case.

## Exit gate (must be true before Phase 9)
- [ ] Sequencing, dependencies, risk, business case documented
- [ ] Frontier moves planned (each with trigger + assurance precondition) **[+I]**
- [ ] Human transition planned (reskilling, role change) **[+I]**
